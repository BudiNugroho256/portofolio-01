ABOUT US


GoLitech was founded in 1996 from a group of students who like to play games. They plan to make their own equipment without buying it outright. in the early days they struggled because no one was interested in their gaming equipment. several times they often fought because of the loss of business sales until finally the GoLitech founding team disbanded. in the end GoLitech is fully owned by its founder now.


In 2004, GoLitech began to stand again under the leadership of the only founder. The bitter experience he had felt allowed the founder to overcome all obstacles. GoLitech was almost bankrupt, but fortunately it was able to overcome it. All the hard work has finally paid off with GoLitech becoming one of the leaders in the gaming kit industry for computers with stores all over the world.
